Australia's Rise as a Space Nation

How Australia transformed from a minor participant into a rapidly growing space nation after 2018.

For decades, Australia played only a small role in the global space industry. While other nations launched astronauts, built satellites, and established agencies, Australia's efforts remained fragmented.

That changed dramatically in 2018 with the creation of the Australian Space Agency.

For decades, Australia remained largely absent from the global space industry. But after the establishment of the Australian Space Agency in 2018, the country's ambitions rapidly expanded.
